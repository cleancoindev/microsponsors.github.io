Low Fees

  • Creators + Publishers: no fee for registration.
  • Sponsors + Media Buyers: no fee for bidding.
  • When a winning bid is selected by the seller, Microsponsors charges a fee of 1.5% to each side of the sale.

Instant, Flexible Payouts

  • Instant transaction settlement. No waiting around!
  • Cash-out your earnings any time.
  • Get paid in US-dollar stablecoins like $USDC or $DAI, or any other custom ERC-20 compatible token you like (such as a community or personal token).

Transparent

  • Transparent, verifiable auctions help Sponsors get the best ROI, and Creators + Publishers get the right price.
  • No hidden fees or deceptive pricing. Fees are clear as you use our Exchange and are detailed in the FAQ.

Fair Marketplace

  • Microsponsors is a fair, neutral marketplace that helps eliminate waste, fraud and deceptive practices.
  • Our mission is to deliver top-tier monetization solutions on a level playing field for businesses and individuals alike, no matter the size.

Tamper-Proof

  • Publishers + Creators are digitally verified with a tamper-proof blockchain Registry.
  • Auction bids are secured with cryptographic signature tools.
